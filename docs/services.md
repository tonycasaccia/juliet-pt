## What to Expect

Sessions are fully individualized and focus on restoring function, reducing pain, and improving movement quality.

Treatment may include:

- Manual therapy
- Neuromuscular re-education
- Strength and conditioning
- Sport-specific rehabilitation
- Movement retraining and coordination work

---

## What We Treat

### Cervical Spine

Headache, neck musculotendinous or ligamentous strains or sprains, instability in the cervical spine (CCI, etc.), cervical radiculopathy, disc herniation, arthritis, stenosis, whiplash, concussion

### Thoracic Spine

Disc injury, ribcage associated dysfunction, scoliosis

### Upper Extremity

Rotator cuff impingement, tear or repair, shoulder instability or dislocation, frozen shoulder / adhesive capsulitis, elbow injury (golfers elbow, tennis elbow, etc.), carpal tunnel syndrome

### Lumbar Spine

Lumbar radiculopathy, stenosis, disc injury, scoliosis, unspecified back pain

### Lower Extremity

Hip: impingement, arthritis and spurring, bursitis, tendonitis / tendinopathy, labral tear  
Knee: ligamentous injury (ACL, etc.), meniscus, patellar dislocation  
Ankle: sprains, strains, bunion, tendonitis / tendinopathy, Achilles and calf injury, plantar fasciitis

### Other

Vertigo, concussion, TMJ, pelvic health, tailbone injury, airway focused training, strength & conditioning programming, post-operative rehabilitation

## Aquatic Therapy Program

A specialized aquatic physical therapy program combining advanced rehabilitation techniques with the therapeutic properties of water to support recovery and improve function.

### Benefits

- Reduced joint stress and pain
- Improved strength and flexibility
- Enhanced balance and mobility
- Improved cardiovascular function
- Faster recovery
- Suitable for all ages and fitness levels

Sessions are offered in group settings or one-on-one (30 or 60 minutes).
